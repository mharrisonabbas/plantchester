01 GRUB Food Fair

GRUB is Manchester's secret vegan streetfood haven based at The Red Bank Project in Cheetham Hill. Open Wednesday to Sunday, this chilled, dog-friendly hangout with rotating food vendors and craft beers will simply blow your mind. Perfect for a weekend bite.

Visit Website

02 Awakening Compassion

Awakening Compassion is a friendly outreach group based in Manchester, who hosts events every Sunday from 1pm-4pm on Market Street. Offering leaflets, placards and virtual reality headsets to spark conversations about Veganism and invite friendly discussions. Got some plant-based snacks to share? Bring them along and join the good vibes.

Visit Website

03 Vegan and Animal Rights Conference

Get stuck into the cause by attending the Vegan and Animal Rights Conference 2025, which hits Manchester from 29-20 March at the Mercure Piccadilly Hotel. Expect inspiring talks, hands-on workshops and panels with 60+ experts. Join 400+ advocates, connect and level up your activism.
